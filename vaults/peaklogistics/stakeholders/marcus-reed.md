# Marcus Reed - CEO, PeakLogistics

## Role
Chief Executive Officer and Co-founder

## Background
Former VP of Operations at a major freight logistics company. Saw firsthand how inefficient the matching process was between suppliers with shipments and carriers with available capacity. Co-founded PeakLogistics with Sarah to solve this problem with technology.

## Goals
What is Marcus trying to achieve? What does success look like for him?

- **Launch PLM in 6 weeks** - Aggressive timeline to capture market opportunity before competitors
- **Prove the marketplace model** - Demonstrate that suppliers and shippers will actually use the platform
- **Secure Series A funding** - Successful launch will unlock next funding round
- **Move fast without breaking things** - Speed matters, but quality can't suffer
- **Build sustainable competitive advantage** - First-mover advantage in this space is critical

## Challenges / Pains
What obstacles, frustrations, or pain points is Marcus dealing with? What keeps him up at night?

- **6-week timeline is aggressive** - Knows this is tight, but market window is closing
- **Funding runway** - Current funding runs through Q2 2026, need traction before then
- **Competition** - Two other startups are building similar platforms
- **Trust and adoption** - Marketplace is only valuable if both sides adopt it
- **Regulatory complexity** - Logistics industry has compliance requirements that can't be ignored

## Needs / Wants
What does Marcus need from Loka?

- **Clear visibility into progress** - Weekly check-ins on what's done, what's at risk
- **Proactive problem-solving** - Don't want to hear about problems after they've derailed the timeline
- **Options, not just blockers** - When something's at risk, present 2-3 options with trade-offs
- **Direct communication** - Values transparency and speed over polish
- **Realistic timelines** - Better to know it's 7 weeks than miss a 6-week commitment

## Communication Preferences
- **Prefers:** Slack for quick updates, video calls for complex discussions
- **Cadence:** Weekly status update, ad-hoc for blockers
- **Style:** Direct, data-driven, action-oriented
- **Decision speed:** Fast - values 80% solution now over 100% solution later
